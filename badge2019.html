Badge User Manual



The badge by default boots into LED Mode. Presing the keys will change the key light patterns, and colors of the neopixels. Placing your hands 2 - 25 cm above the left and right sides of the badge will modify the brightness and color of the neopixels.

Pressing the top of the rotary encoder (it will click when you have activated the button) will change into sound mode. The left distance sensor will affect volume, and the right sensor will change pitch. Pressing the keys will change waveforms. Rotating the rotary encoder will change the range of pitch that the right sensor will modify.
